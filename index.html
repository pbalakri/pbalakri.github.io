Who are we?

We are a company that focuses on the basics of everything we do - including how we make money, how we approach problems, how we communicate solutions, how we hire, how we pay, etc.

Clarity

We are aware that a problem stated in words does not paint the whole picture. We understand the problem from multiple perspectives to fully understand the dimensionality of the problem. Then and only then, do we start designing a solution.

Speed

We recognize that in this world of ridiculously fast electric cars and 10 minute delivery, the time between an idea to its implementation should tend towards 0. This is also what makes our work exciting.

Elegance

Our solutions are elegant - even the hacks that we do. Elegance in our opinion is a balance of speed, longevity of the solution, and how proud we feel in its aftermath.
